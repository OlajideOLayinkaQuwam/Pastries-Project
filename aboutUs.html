Hey there! I’m Abdulyekini Rahmot Adeola, the face and hands behind Raardel Pastries and Event. I’ve always loved seeing people smile over something sweet whether it’s a slice of cake, a box of pastries, or a beautifully set-up event. What started as a small passion for baking quickly turned into something much bigger creating memorable moments that people hold onto. At Rardel Pastries and Event, we blend the joy of baking with the art of event decoration. From freshly made pastries to full event setups, everything is done with care, creativity, and attention to detail. No matter the occasion birthdays, weddings, or just a reason to celebrate I’m here to make it warm, beautiful, and full of flavor. Because for me, it’s not just about pastries or decorations… It’s about creating joy, one sweet moment at a time.
